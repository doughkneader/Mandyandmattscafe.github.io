Our Story

More than a menu, we've created a vibe.

Welcome to Mandy & Matt's Cafe, where we're bringing back to life the spirit of a Portland favorite, Kathy & Dave's Cafe. With three decades of experience in her family's beloved business, Mandy, alongside her husband Matt, is continuing a legacy of family atmosphere, friendly service, and terrific, home-cooked food.

Our mission is simple: to be a warm, inviting, community-focused hub. We prioritize sustainable practices and exceptional customer service, from our classic breakfast dishes to our famous family muffin recipes. We're not just a new venture; we are the revival of a cherished local institution, and we invite you to be a part of our story.

We believe in good food, good company, and the power of community. We look forward to welcoming you.
